General Guidlines

Use at least normal difficulty
Wear a helmet when mining. If it breaks you must leave immediately
Dug out mineshafts must be 3x3 with wooden supports each 10 blocks
If you find a bucket, you may use it regardless of the stage you are in
Pre-spawned villages do not count towards requirements. You can still use them however

Stage 1

  1. You must have a protected area with a wheat farm
  2. You must have some sort of tree farm (it can just be a small forest)
